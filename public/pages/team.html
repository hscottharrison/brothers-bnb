Our Team

After 10 years of working in construction, my brother and I made the decision to buy Brothers B and B. We have now successfully woned and operated our company for over 16 years. During those years we have done ground up, commercial remodels, tenant finish outs, medical, office warehouse, manufacturing, light industrial and food industry. Our company is completely family owned and operated bringing a personal touch to an otherwise impersonal industry. We have return clients stretching back to our beginnings and take pride in every project that we do.
